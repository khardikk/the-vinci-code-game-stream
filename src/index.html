INSTRUCTIONS

    How to Play:

    Enter Your Name:

  • When prompted, enter your name.

    • Menu Options:

  • Start New Game:
    Begin a new game and try to memorize and enter sequences of random numbers.
  • See Leaderboard:
    Check out the leaderboard.
  • Update Name:
    Change your displayed name.

    • Gameplay:

  • Start a New Game to initiate the memory challenge.
  • Memorize and enter the sequence of numbers shown to you.
  • Successfully completing a level advances you to the next with an additional number.

    • Scoring:

  • Your final score is the highest level you reach before making a mistake.
